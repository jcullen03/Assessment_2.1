About

Josephine Turner has always had a love for baking, a passion that grew into a lifelong pursuit of creating unique, artisanal treats. As a professional baker with years of experience, she honed her skills in renowned bakeries, perfecting her craft in custom cakes, pastries, and breads. Now, Josephine is turning her dream into reality with her own home-based bakery, Turner's Treats, based in the heart of Melbourne. Inspired by the joy that a beautifully crafted dessert can bring, she launched Turner's Treats to offer her local community delicious, handmade baked goods with a personal touch. Whether you're looking for a custom celebration cake or a warm loaf of fresh bread, Josephine's creations are made with love, care, and a dedication to quality that sets her apart.
